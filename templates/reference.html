Instructions for using the application

Address Book

  • Address Book is useful for adding your contacts

  • This addressbook is presented as list of contacts with contact`s name, phone and his/her birthday. In the top of this there is search area, where you can enter your input to find contact by name or surname. "All" button is reseting all filters

  • In the right side top-corner you can see 3 interaction buttons: Delete, View and Update. It represents actions that was mentioned above

Note Book

  • Note Book can help you with adding notes

  • Note book is represented as list of notes. It contains info about note`s title, description and its tags. Tags are made as an interaction button, that add to filter following tags. Also it consists the same buttons as addressbook: Delete and Update. In the top of this there is search area, where you can enter your input to find note by its title

  • In the left side there is side-bar with tags existing in your notes. Using it you can filter your list of notes

News

  • Information section of the application. In this section news feeds are divided into popular categories: World news, Financial news, Sports news, Entertainment news and TechCrunch news.

  • Active news headlines with the ability to go to a detailed article on the main page of the source.

Weather

  • In the upper right corner of the News section, a brief weather forecast for your location is constantly displayed (the location is determined by the application automatically).

  • For a full weather forecast for your location, click on the "see more >>" link under the weather forecast.

  • On the weather forecast page, it is possible to get a weather forecast for any city of the world interests the user. Enter the name of the city of interest in "another location" and click on the search button.

Currency Converter

  • On the page of the Currency Converter, the scrolling line indicates the current exchange rates to the base currency - the Euro.

  • The Currency Converter has a selections of all the valid world currencies.

  • To convert currencies, choose in the selection one currency, enter an amount and select another currency in the next selection. To get the result, click "Convert".
